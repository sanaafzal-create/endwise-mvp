Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Can't find what you're looking for? Use the contact form above — we'll get back to you.

When will EndWise be available?
+
EndWise is currently in active development. We're conducting founder-led interviews with families, hospice teams, and estate professionals to ensure the platform truly meets the needs of those it serves. Join our waitlist to get early access and be the first to know when we launch.
Is EndWise free to use?
+
We're committed to equity — we believe all families deserve clarity and support regardless of their financial situation. Our early access program will be free. Pricing details for premium features will be shared closer to launch. Our core mission will always remain accessible.
Is my personal information and family data secure?
+
Absolutely. EndWise is built with enterprise-grade encryption from the ground up. Your documents, your tasks, and your family's information are encrypted end-to-end. You control who has access — and you can revoke access at any time. We will never sell your data.
I'm interested in partnering with or advising EndWise. How do I reach out?
+
We're actively looking for values-aligned advisors in estate law, tech-for-good, and grief care — as well as VC firms and angel investors who believe in equitable, compassionate technology. Use the contact form above and select "Partnership or Investment Inquiry," or email us directly at partners@endwise.com.
Does EndWise support veterans and military families?
+
Yes. Veterans and military families have unique needs — burial honors, VA benefits, specific documentation requirements. EndWise includes dedicated guidance and resources for veterans and their families, respecting their service with the dignity it deserves. Reach out at community@endwise.com for specific questions.
I'm currently going through a loss. Can EndWise help me right now?
+
We're so sorry for your loss. While EndWise is still in development, please reach out to us directly at hello@endwise.com — our team will personally point you toward trusted resources for your immediate needs. You don't have to figure this out alone, and we will not leave you without support.

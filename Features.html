Closure Compass – Dashboard

A personalized path to help you move forward, one step at a time.

The Closure Compass guides you through what needs attention next, based on your unique situation — your location, your family structure, and the estate you're navigating. It breaks complex responsibilities into clear, manageable steps so you're never overwhelmed, never lost. You don't have to do everything today. Just the next right thing.

Document Vault

A secure place to store everything that matters most.

The Document Vault helps you safely store and organize essential records so they're easy to find when you need them — and accessible to the people you trust. Wills, death certificates, power of attorney, property titles — everything in one encrypted, private space. No more searching through boxes or losing critical paperwork.

Emotional Support

Grief doesn't follow a schedule. You're allowed to pause.

This space is here to support you emotionally — without pressure, without expectations. Whether you want to reflect, remember, or simply take a quiet moment, you can move at your own pace. Write in your grief journal, read today's peace note, or visit the Memory Wall to share a story. There's no right way to grieve. There's only what feels right for you.

Pre – Planning

Give your family the gift of clarity and peace.

Pre-planning allows you to organize your wishes, documents, and information in advance — so your loved ones never have to face confusion in their most painful moments. Document your burial preferences, write letters to the people you love, and share access with your executor. The greatest act of care you can give is clarity.
